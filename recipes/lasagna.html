Ingredients

  • Meat

  • Lasagna Sheets

Directions

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Cook meat, onion and garlic over medium heat until well browned
  3. Stir in tomato products as well as water and add seasoning, stirring occasionally
  4. In a pot of boiling water, cook the lasagna sheets until perfect
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese with egg, 2 tablespoons of parsley and half a teaspoon of salt
  6. Preheat the oven
  7. Assemble everything in layers as per the image
  8. Bake in oven covered for 25 minutes and uncovered for 25 minutes
